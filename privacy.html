Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

HUNTX is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit huntx.co (the "Site") or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Name, email address, shipping/billing address, and phone number during checkout or account creation.
  • Payment information (handled securely by Stripe—see below).
  • Any information you submit via contact forms, community requests, or reviews.

We also collect automatically: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and device info for site improvement and security.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Process orders and fulfill shipments.
  • Communicate about your purchases, account, or requests (e.g., Summit access).
  • Improve our site, products, and services.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure site security.

Security

We utilize industry-standard security measures, including military-grade encryption technology (SSL/TLS) and secure servers, to protect your personal and financial information. Payments are processed entirely by Stripe, a trusted PCI-compliant provider—we never store full credit card details on our servers.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only with:

  • Service providers (e.g., Stripe for payments, shipping carriers like USPS/UPS).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at support@huntx.co. We will respond within a reasonable time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.

Questions? Contact us at support@huntx.co.
