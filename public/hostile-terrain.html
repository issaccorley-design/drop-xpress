Preparing for Hostile Terrain: Essential Systems

From blistering deserts to choking jungles to oxygen-starved peaks — hostile terrain doesn't forgive poor preparation.

1. Environmental Threat Matrix

Every terrain type has a primary killer. Match your systems to the threat:

  • Desert (Heat + Dehydration) — 50°C days, 0°C nights, <5% humidity
  • Jungle (Humidity + Pathogens) — 95–100% humidity, constant rain, insect vectors
  • High Altitude (Hypoxia + Cold) — <50% oxygen above 5,000 m, sudden storms
  • Arctic / Sub-Zero — -50°C wind chill, frostbite in minutes
