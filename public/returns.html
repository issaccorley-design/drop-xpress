Returns & Refund Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

At HUNTX, we stand behind our gear. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to make it right.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

You may return most unused, new-condition items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligibility

  • Item must be unworn, unused, in original packaging with all tags/labels attached.
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) required.
  • Personalized/custom items and final-sale products are non-returnable (noted at checkout if applicable).

How to Return

  1. Contact us at support@huntx.co with your order number and reason for return within 30 days.
  2. We will provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) and return shipping instructions.
  3. Ship the item back to us (customer pays return shipping unless defective/damaged on arrival).
  4. Once received and inspected, we will issue a refund to your original payment method (typically within 5–10 business days).

Defective or Damaged Items

If an item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 7 days with photos. We will cover return shipping and provide a replacement or full refund.

Refunds

Refunds exclude original shipping fees. Sale/clearance items may be eligible for store credit only in some cases.

Questions? Email support@huntx.co.
