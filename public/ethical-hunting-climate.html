Ethical Hunting in Changing Climates

Climate shifts migration, shrinks habitat, and pressures populations. Ethical hunters adapt — or stop.

1. Observed Changes & Impacts

  • Shifting migration — Earlier springs push elk/deer to higher elevations sooner
  • Habitat compression — Droughts shrink water sources, concentrate game
  • Population stress — Warmer winters reduce winter kill but increase disease

2. Ethical Adaptation Strategies

  1. Adjust seasons & zones — Follow data from state wildlife agencies
  2. Selective harvest — Prioritize mature males, spare young females
  3. Habitat support — Participate in conservation easements, water guzzler projects
