Modular Gear for Long-Duration Autonomy

When resupply is impossible and extraction is 14–30 days out, your loadout must be self-contained, redundant, and ruthlessly efficient.

1. Core Principles of True Autonomy

  • Layered redundancy — Two independent water sources, three fire-starting methods, dual comms
  • Modular attachment ecosystem — MOLLE, G-Hook, or magnetic quick-release for field reconfiguration
  • Weight hierarchy — 60% torso (main pack), 30% hips (belt kit), 10% limbs (pockets)
  • Multi-role gear only — Poncho liner = insulation + camouflage + water collector
