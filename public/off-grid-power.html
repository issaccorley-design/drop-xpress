Off-Grid Power Solutions for Extended Missions

When the grid is 1,000 miles away and solar panels freeze solid, you need power that doesn't quit.

1. Power Budget Reality Check

Typical 28-day mission devices:

  • Satellite messenger: 100–200 mAh/day
  • GPS: 300–500 mAh/day
  • Headlamp: 50–100 mAh/night
  • Phone (low-power mode): 200–400 mAh/day

Total daily draw: 1,000–2,000 mAh. Plan for 30–50% buffer.

2. Tiered Power Generation Stack

  1. Primary: Solar (20–40W foldable) — 100–200Wh/day in good sun
  2. Secondary: Hand-crank / kinetic — 5–10Wh/hour manual effort
  3. Tertiary: Thermoelectric (body heat) — 1–3Wh/hour in cold
  4. Backup: Spare 18650/21700 cells — Rotate warm spares
